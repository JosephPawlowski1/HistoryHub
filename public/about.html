About History United

History-Hub, will be able to let students register with their school (Marquette), where they will be able to create or join events that are affiliated with their applied or interested major (History). Events can be defined as University events that the school is offering, off-campus events, or study groups. Students will also be able to join/create classes that they are registered for. Current students will also be able to join/create events, such as creating a study group or posting an event to meet up on or off campus.

History-Hub, will be able to let professors register with their school (Marquette) credentials, where they will be able to create or/and join events, create classes, and post blogs of stories of there experience in their field of study/major. Teachers will also be able to create classes that they are registered to teach the current semester for students in the class to join. Professors will be able to create and/or read posts that can benefit a student who is confused about course material and gain a better understanding. Professors can also read/post blogs for students to help gain networking connections from there applied major and help give important insight/experience to students.
