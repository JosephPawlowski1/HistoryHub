Why Major in History? Read More:
Students and parents often ask some variation of this question: “What can you do with a History degree?” As historians, we honestly believe the answer is: “What can’t you do with a History degree?”
What Can You Do With A Major In History? Read More:
About a third of our graduates become secondary school teachers, while a number of others earn advanced degrees from the best graduate programs in the country. Others go to law and medical schools, or go directly to work in industry, business, non-profits and many other fields.
Available Internships in History Read More:
The History Department has formed off- and on-campus partnerships to provide professional experiences for History majors and minors with the following institutions and departments. Some internships are paid; most can be taken for credit. Not all internships are available every semester. Interested students should contact J. Patrick Mullins, Ph.D., Public History Director, at john.mullins@marquette.edu.
